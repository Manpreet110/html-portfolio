The Best Movies According to Manpreet

My top 3 movies of all time.

Sanam Teri Kasam

This is a romantic tragedy about Saru, a bookish girl disowned by her family,
and Inder, a rebellious yet kind-hearted man. As Inder helps
Saru transform her life, love blossoms between them.
However, fate has other plans, leading to a heartbreaking ending.

3 Idiots

This movie follows three engineering students—Rancho, Farhan, and Raju—challenging the rigid education system.
Rancho’s unconventional wisdom inspires his friends to follow their dreams,
while a mystery about his disappearance unfolds. The film blends humor, friendship, and life lessons.

4 Sahibzaade

This movie is an animated historical film depicting the bravery and sacrifice of Guru Gobind Singh Ji’s four sons,
who stood against the Mughal empire to protect Sikhism.
